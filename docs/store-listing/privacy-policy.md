# NederUrdu Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-18

NederUrdu is a Dutch learning app for Urdu-speaking beginners.

## Information We Collect
NederUrdu does not require an account and does not ask for personal information.

The app saves learning progress locally on your device so lessons can continue where you left off. This local progress is not sent to NederUrdu servers.

## Internet Access
The Android app includes internet permission so the web-based learning experience can support standard browser features and future hosted resources if needed. The current learning content is packaged inside the app.

## Third Parties
NederUrdu does not include advertising, analytics, or third-party account login in this version.

## Children
NederUrdu is an educational app. If children use the app, a parent or guardian should decide whether it is suitable for them.

## Contact
For privacy questions, contact the NederUrdu app owner.

## Changes
This policy may be updated when the app changes. The effective date will be updated when that happens.
