# NederUrdu Google Play Listing Draft

## App Name
NederUrdu

## Short Description
Learn practical Dutch with Urdu support.

## Full Description
NederUrdu helps Urdu-speaking beginners learn practical Dutch for daily life in the Netherlands.

The app starts from A0 basics and grows into A1 and A2 lessons for real situations like greetings, family, home, transport, health, shopping, school, work, appointments, forms, and simple communication.

Features:
- Urdu-first explanations for beginner Dutch learners
- A0, A1, and A2 lesson paths
- Dutch-to-Urdu and Urdu-to-Dutch practice
- Sentence-building exercises with Dutch word tiles
- Urdu hints for harder practice questions
- Dutch letter practice with pronunciation support
- Local progress saving on your device
- Works offline after installation
- No account required

NederUrdu is designed as a simple learning companion for people who want to build useful Dutch step by step.

## Privacy Summary
NederUrdu does not require an account and does not collect personal information. Lesson progress is saved locally on the user's device.
